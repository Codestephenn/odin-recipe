Hot Dog Lasagna

Description

Hot lasagna is a fun and creative twist on the classic italian dish. This family-friendly recipe features layers of cooked lasagna noodles, marinara sauce, sliced hot dogs and goey cheese. It is a simple yet satisfying meal that's sure to please both kids and adult alike.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Hot Dogs
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesam Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°f(190°c)
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a baking dish, spread a thine layer of marinara sauce on the bottom.
  4. Arrange a layer of cooked lasagna over the sauce.
  5. Place sliced hot dogs evenly over the noodles.
  6. Spoon marinara sauce over the hot dog covering them completely.
  7. Sprinkle shredded mozzarell cheese over the sauce.
  8. Repeat the layer until all ingredients are used, ending with the layer of cheese on top.
  9. Cover the baking dish with aluminium foil and bake in preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly
  10. Remove foil and continue baking for additional 5-10 minutes, or until cheese is golden brown.
  11. Let the hot dog lasagna cool for few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
