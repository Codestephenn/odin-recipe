Chicken Stir-fry

Description

Chicken stir-fry is flavourful and nutritious dish that is quick and easy to prepare. This versatile recipe combines tender strips of chicken wtith colorful assortment of vegetables all, cooked in a savory sauce.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast(cut into strips)
  • Bell Peppers(sliced)
  • Brocolli florets
  • Carrots(Sliced)
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Garlic(mince)
  • Ginger(grated)
  • Cornstarch
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken strips in a soy sauce, mimce garlic, grated ginger and cornstarch for about 30 minutes.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or larhe skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Stir-fry the marinated chicken until it is cooked through and golden brown. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  4. In the same pan, add a bit more oil if needed and stir-fry the sliced bell peppers, broccoli florets, and carrots until they are crisp-tender.
  5. Return the cooked chicken to the pan and mix everything together.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Drizzle with sesame oil for extral flavor
  8. Serve hot over steamed rice or noodles. Enjoy your delicious chicken stir-fry!
